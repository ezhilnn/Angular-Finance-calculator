FINANCIAL CALCULATORS

CAGR Calculator

Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) is a financial metric used to measure the average annual growth rate of an investment or business over a specified period, smoothing out fluctuations. It represents the consistent rate at which an investment would have grown yearly, assuming it grew at a steady rate.

SIP Calculator

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a disciplined investment approach where individuals invest fixed amounts at regular intervals, typically monthly, in mutual funds. It enables long-term wealth creation by averaging out market fluctuations, leveraging the power of compounding, and encouraging consistent savings habits for financial growth.

Position Sizing Calculator

A Position Sizing Calculator is a tool used in trading and investing to determine the appropriate size or number of units or shares to purchase or sell based on predefined risk parameters such as account size, risk tolerance, entry price, stop-loss, and desired risk percentage per trade. It helps traders manage risk by calculating position sizes aligned with their risk management strategy.

LUMPSUM FV Calculator

A Lump Sum Future Value (FV) Calculator is a financial tool used to estimate the future value of a lump sum investment or deposit based on the initial amount invested, the interest rate, and the time period it's invested for. It calculates the projected value at maturity, factoring in compound interest or returns earned on the lump sum over time..
